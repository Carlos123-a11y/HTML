recipe for a brownie

  • Flour: All-purpose flour provides structure and helps bind the ingredients together.
  • Sugar: Granulated sugar is a common choice, but some recipes also use brown sugar for a chewier, moister brownie.
  • Cocoa Powder: This is essential for the chocolate flavor and color.
  • Eggs: Eggs contribute to the richness and structure of the brownies.
  • Butter: Butter adds richness and moisture, and can be used melted or softened.
  • Vanilla Extract: Vanilla enhances the chocolate flavor.
  • Chocolate Chips: These can be added for extra chocolate flavor and texture.
